Author: Katrina Pietroniro

This public-safe release contains the reviewed publication layer for Governance Omega Package 03.

Contents include:
- a status-controlled citizen-action registry
- a petition and consultation channel crosswalk
- an institutional outreach register
- a coalition and community participation index
- a public-education and communication register
- a response and outcome ledger
- a claim-validation matrix
- participation safeguards
- visual governance maps
- public-safe registry workbook

Evidence-state rule:

AUTHOR -> SEND -> DELIVER -> RECEIVE -> ACKNOWLEDGE -> RESPOND -> IMPLEMENT -> AUDIT -> ARCHIVE

These states are not interchangeable. A draft is not a sent submission. A sent message is not proof of external receipt. A petition signature does not establish presentation or policy adoption. Publication proves dissemination, not factual validation.

Public boundary:
- recipient identities are redacted
- family and minor records are excluded
- unredacted correspondence and controlled evidence are excluded
- serious allegations are not represented as findings
- self-reported participation counts retain explicit qualification
- proposed charters and standards are identified as proposals, not enacted law
