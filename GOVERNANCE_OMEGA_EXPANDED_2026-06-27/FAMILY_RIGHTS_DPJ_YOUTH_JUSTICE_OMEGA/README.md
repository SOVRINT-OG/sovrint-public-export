A complaint is not a finding. A source-located pleading is not proof of filing. A hearing report is not an official hearing record. A decision is not implementation. A legal crosswalk identifies possible relevance but does not prove a case-specific violation.

All charters and standards are identified as proposed governance instruments, not enacted law. This release is governance documentation and research support; it is not legal advice, an adjudication or a finding of wrongdoing.
