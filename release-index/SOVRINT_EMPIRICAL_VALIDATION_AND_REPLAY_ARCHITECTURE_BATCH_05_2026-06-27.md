## Evidence boundary

The source candidate `II = 0.98` threshold is preserved. A synthetic sensitivity result cannot replace or recalibrate it. Empirical promotion requires controlled observed data, defined units, measurement uncertainty, independent ground truth, competent governance review, and external replication.
